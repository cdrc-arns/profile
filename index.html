VARDAE

Dj and producer from Lyon

BIOGRAPHY

Vardae is a project with the ambition of breaking away from the genre barrier, to explore news sounds territory around the love of psychedelic music. A powerful sound which navigates from dub to tribe, passing through techno and drum’n’bass. A universe where immersion and experience prevail over codes with the idea of offering a journey to each of its appearances.
